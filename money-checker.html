How weeks are counted
Budget weeks run Sunday → Saturday. The current partial week is included but reduced by whatever you've already spent. The final week of the budget month is pro-rated — only the days up to (but not including) the pay-in date are counted, at a daily rate of £17.86/day.

How the reserve is calculated
Full weeks count as £125 each. The final week of the budget month is pro-rated by day — days from its Sunday to the day before pay-in, at £125 ÷ 7 per day. The current week is further reduced by whatever you've already spent. Leave "spent this week" blank to count the full remaining allocation.
