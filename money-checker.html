How weeks are counted
Budget weeks run Sunday → Saturday. The current partial week counts as one allocation. Weeks are shown for the current budget month — from the last pay-in to the day before the next one.

How the reserve is calculated
Each Sunday opens a new £125 weekly budget. The app counts how many Sundays (including the current partial week) remain before the next pay-in date, then multiplies by your weekly budget to give your required reserve. If your balance exceeds that, the difference is your headroom — money free for other account spending. If it falls short, that's your deficit.
