How On Budget works
Enter your account balance once at the start of each month. Log spends as you go — tag them Weekly Budget (comes off your £125/week allowance) or Other (comes off your balance but doesn't touch the weekly allowance). The dashboard shows your remaining pot and how much headroom you have above the weekly reserve needed to last until pay-in.
