How On Budget works
Enter your account balance once at the start of each month. Log spends as you go — tag them Food (comes off your £125/week allowance), Travel (comes off a separate monthly travel budget), or Other (comes off your balance but isn't reserved for — doesn't affect headroom). The dashboard shows your remaining pot and how much headroom you have above the combined food + travel reserve needed to last until pay-in.
